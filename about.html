Who Are we

Elegancia IFM – Delivering essential and sustainable integrated facilities management services.

Elegancia Facility Management offers a service that will meet all of your expectations and where possible exceed them. Our team is focused
on quality so that you are able to concentrate on your core business. We pride ourselves on being able to offer a wide range of facilities management services from one point of contact and strive to work with you to develop a service level agreement that not only reflects the high standards of your organization but also meets your businesses core requirements.

Vision, Mission & Values

Vision

At Elegancia our vision is to build long term relationships with our clients by providing exceptional services and quality products through our company’s innovative framework and keen adherence to trust and loyalty.

Mision

Elegancia’s mission is to consistently be driven by a dedicated team that provides quality services and products that exceed our clients’ expectations.

Core Values

“Supporting the Vision” of our customers - it is the spirit of who and what we are! Our pursuit of the vision governs the way we make everyday decisions and is manifest in our values of:

Safety - We believe Safety must be at the forefront of all our decision-making.

Collaboration - We work together, share ideas and recognize the contribution of others.

Integrity - We are honest and ethical, and treat others with fairness, dignity and respect.

Excellence - We recognize the importance of excellence in ourselves and in the delivery of services of the highest quality.

Accountability - We make take personal ownership of tasks, are responsible for our actions and deliver on our deadlines.

Professionalism- We are always professional; doing what is right, not what is easy.

Adaptability - We move with speed, dexterity innovative ways to adapt and adjust to emerging client and project requirements.

Compassion - We care about our customers, our employees and their families, and the communities in which we operate.

Why favor us?

We will tailor our services at your location to reflect the high standards of your organization. We will provide a team of engineers led by our assigned Contract Manager, which fulfil your standard requirements and respond to any developments that may occur.

We appreciate you are looking for a partner who can deal with all maintenance related issues. Our scope of service, coverage and self-delivery means we are able to fulfil all of your requirements, whatever they are within the M&E and building maintenance field.

We will include the provision of a responsive service that deals with any issue appropriately, including any specialist assets or requirements. Our experience elsewhere means we already have in place the knowhow, processes and resource to deal successfully with any developments. We want to ensure you are delighted with the service.

Given the services you are considering as part of a potential contract it is vital that you are confident in, and can demonstrate to those around you, the success of the service. This confidence comes from the ability to see clearly, through information provided, the level the service is being delivered to, what incidents have occurred and how they were responded to and what is planned for the future.

The ability to scale back or increase the scope and breadth of services is vital to the successful delivery of the contract. Given our size, the ability to deliver flexible services is one of our key strengths. Flexing our service can mean adding other properties to the contract as much as reducing reactive costs if necessary.

We manage contracts through a collaborative relationship with our customers, with honest and open communication that enables us to work together for the best possible outcomes. We have a highly experienced team, enabling us to utilize key lessons learned to improve the service we provide.

We will ensure your demands and expectations are met with a well-resourced team. Understandably there is a focus on cost and where this can be reduced; but not at the expense of service quality.

We can offer services and when you need them, whether this is managing specialist assets or providing a single helpdesk for all queries or fulfilling project work such as office moves, new asset installation or something else.
