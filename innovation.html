One Size Doesn't Fit All
Our Integrated Service
Delivery Model

Organisations are doing a lot more for less cost. The need to increase productivity and efficiency is a key driver in Facilities Management (FM) market. An Integrated Service Delivery Model can provide an independent matrix of processes, resources, skills and knowledge to manage the delivery of FM services. Regardless of the delivery model, whether the services are managed by in-house teams or are outsourced, the Integrated FM Delivery Partner concept can embed an integrated approach for the streamlined delivery of FM services.

Elegancia's service is scalable to suit your needs - the process begins with the development of a sourcing strategy and the mobilisation of an independent supply chain, with ongoing day-to-day FM delivery. You can employ the whole model from end-to-end, or select elements that are best suited to your particular needs. Elegancia has the ability to deal with reactive events, issue planned work, monitor BMS systems and ensure that FM services are delivered across the organisation, all through a single integrated system, thus ensuring legal and contract compliance is achieved.
